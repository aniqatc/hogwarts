Hogwarts

School of Wizardry and Witchcraft

Founded in the 10th century by Godric Gryffindor, Rowena Ravenclaw, Helga Hufflepuff and Salazar Slytherin, Hogwarts was established in the Highlands of Scotland to educate young wizards and witches as well as to keep students safe from muggle persecution. Theory has it that Rowena Ravenclaw came up with the name of Hogwarts after dreaming of a warty hog that led her to a cliff by a lake.

Gryffindor

Gryffindor was founded by the wizard Godric Gryffindor. Gryffindor is known by their bravery and righteousness. They correspond with the element of fire which is what the red in their crest represents with the gold representing the fur-coat of a lion. Gryffindor students are known to have strong morals and a sense to do good.

Slytherin

  • Ambitious
  • Cunning
  • Pride
  • Leadership
  • Competitive

Slytherin was founded by the wizard Salazar Slytherin who valued pure-blood students. Because of this, it was rare for a muggle-born student to be sorted in. Slytherin is known for their strong-willed students that have the potential to be strong leaders if they manage to follow the rules. They correspond with the element of water which is represented in their crest with green corresponding with lakes and silver corresponding with grey rainwater. Slytherin students are known to have a predisposition for rule-breaking, cleverness and rivalry with Gryffindor.

Ravenclaw was founded by the mediaval witch Rowena Ravenclaw. Ravenclaw is known by their wits and wisdom. They correspond with the element of air which is what the blue in their crest represents with the brown representing eagle feathers. Ravenclaw students are known to have a predisposition for academics and have a strong curiosity for knowledge.

Hufflepuff

  • Dedicated
  • Hardworking
  • Loyal
  • Patient
  • Modest

Hufflepuff was founded by the witch Helga Hufflepuff. Hufflepuff is known by their wits and wisdom. They correspond with the element of earth which is represented in the crest with yellow corresponding with wheat and black corresponding with soil. Out of all the houses, Hufflepuff is known to be the most inclusive. Hufflepuff students are known to have strong abilities in herbology magic. They are modest and are not as competitive as the other houses.
